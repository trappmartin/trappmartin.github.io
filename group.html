About the Group

We study probabilistic methods to develop robust, reliable, and computationally efficient machine learning. Our group is based at the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Sweden.

Our research spans tractable probabilistic models, Bayesian learning, and probabilistic numerics, with a focus on representing, quantifying, and reducing uncertainties to make machine learning more trustworthy — including efficient and principled approaches for large-scale models such as LLMs and VLMs.
